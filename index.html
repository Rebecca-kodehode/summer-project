What Is Fourth Wing?

Fourth Wing is a fantasy novel by Rebecca Yarros set in a deadly war college where only the strongest survive. Students train to become dragon riders, and danger waits at every corner — from brutal training to deadly secrets. The story follows Violet Sorrengail, a girl who wasn’t meant to be a rider, as she battles fate, enemies, and even her own doubts.
